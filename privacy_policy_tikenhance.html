# TikEnhance - Privacy Policy Effective Date: July 30, 2025 Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how TikEnhance handles your information. ## 1. No Data Collection TikEnhance does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users. All processing is performed locally on the user's device. ## 2. TikTok Integration The app uses TikTok’s official OAuth service to authenticate users. Access tokens are retrieved and used only to publish content directly to the user’s TikTok account. These tokens are not stored or shared with any external servers. ## 3. Third-Party Services TikEnhance does not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools. All interactions are strictly between the app and TikTok’s authorized services. ## 4. Data Security All OAuth-related data is handled securely in memory during the session and cleared after publishing. Users are responsible for ensuring the security of their local device. ## 5. Policy Updates This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Users will be informed of any changes through the application interface. ## 6. Contact For any privacy-related questions, please contact: support@tikenhance.app Thank you for trusting TikEnhance.
